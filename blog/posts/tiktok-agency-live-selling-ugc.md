<h1>What Is a TikTok Live Agency and How Can It Grow Your Business?</h1>

<div class="post-body">
  <p>TikTok is no longer just a platform for dancing videos and trending sounds. It is now one of the most powerful tools for selling products, building communities, and growing a brand in real time. But most business owners don't know how to use it properly — and that's where a TikTok agency comes in.</p>

  <p>These are the questions people ask most about TikTok Live, UGC content, and agency support. I'm going to answer every one of them in plain language.</p>

  <h2>1. What Does a TikTok Agency Actually Do?</h2>
  <p>A TikTok agency manages your presence on the platform so you don't have to do it alone. That includes strategy, content creation, paid ad campaigns, account management, and — most importantly for sales — TikTok Live sessions. A good agency doesn't just post content. It builds a system around your account that brings in real customers, not just views.</p>

  <h2>2. What Is TikTok Live Selling and How Does It Work?</h2>
  <p>TikTok Live Selling is when you or a host goes live on TikTok to sell products in real time. Viewers can browse your product catalogue, ask questions in the comments, and buy without leaving the app. It's like a modern home shopping channel — except your audience is already there, already engaged, and already in the mood to buy.</p>
  <p>In my opinion, TikTok Live is the most underused sales channel for small and medium businesses right now. The brands that start early will dominate their space.</p>

  <h2>3. Can I Make Real Money From TikTok Live?</h2>
  <p>Yes — and brands are doing it right now. TikTok Live allows you to sell physical products, services, and event tickets directly during a broadcast. With the right product, the right host, and a warmed-up audience, a single live session can generate more revenue than a week of regular posts. The key is consistency and strategy — not luck.</p>

  <h2>4. What Is UGC Content and Why Does My Brand Need It?</h2>
  <p>UGC stands for User-Generated Content. It refers to videos that look like they were made by a real customer or creator — not a brand — talking about or showing your product. These videos perform better than polished ads because people trust them. They feel real. A UGC video that says "I tried this and here's what happened" converts far better than a corporate campaign that says "buy our product."</p>
  <p>If your brand doesn't have UGC content, you are losing sales to competitors who do.</p>

  <h2>5. How Do TikTok Paid Ads Work?</h2>
  <p>TikTok ads are video-first and designed to blend into the feed. You can target by age, location, interest, and behaviour. The most effective TikTok ads look like organic content — they don't feel like advertisements. That's why most brands that run TikTok ads without UGC creative get poor results. The creative is everything.</p>
  <p>At Luxival, we build the ad campaign and the content together so that everything works as a system — not just a boosted post.</p>

  <h2>6. What Is TikTok Account Mentorship?</h2>
  <p>Not every business wants to hand over their account to an agency. Some want to learn how to run it themselves. That's what mentorship is for. We teach you the strategy, the content frameworks, the live session structure, and the ad setup — so you can eventually manage it independently. Knowledge stays in your business.</p>

  <h2>7. How Much Does a TikTok Agency Cost?</h2>
  <p>It depends on what you need. Full account management with live sessions, UGC production, and paid ads is a premium service. But a mentorship programme or a single UGC campaign is a much lower entry point. The right question is not "what does it cost?" — it is "what does it cost me if I don't start now while my competitors are still sleeping on TikTok?"</p>
