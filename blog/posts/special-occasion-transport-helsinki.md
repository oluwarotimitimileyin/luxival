## How much does wedding transport cost in Helsinki?

Wedding transport pricing depends on:
- Number of vehicles and journeys required
- Distance between ceremony, photography locations, and reception
- Duration of hire

**Typical Helsinki wedding package**: €350–€900 depending on complexity.

Book wedding transport **4–6 months in advance** for summer weddings (June–August).

## What about corporate VIP transport in Helsinki?

Helsinki regularly hosts international business delegations and executive travel. Proper VIP ground transport is:

- **Discreet** — no branding on vehicle unless requested
- **Punctual** — flight monitoring, real-time traffic, contingency routing
- **Presentable** — executives in clean, premium vehicles reflect on your company

We handle VIP corporate transport for Helsinki business events and multi-day executive travel across Finland.
