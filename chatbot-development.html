Chatbot Development for Your Website

A chatbot should help visitors find what they need, not frustrate them with unhelpful responses. Luxival builds practical website chatbots designed around real customer questions, service navigation, lead capture, and clear handoff to your team.

Problems This Service Solves

  • Visitors leave without enquiring because they cannot find the right information quickly.
  • Pre-sales questions arrive repeatedly and take team time to answer individually.
  • Leads outside working hours are missed or responded to too slowly.
  • International visitors need support in their own language.
  • Appointment and booking guidance requires back-and-forth that a structured flow could handle.

What You Get

  • Conversation design based on your real services and most common customer questions.
  • Customer support flows to answer frequent questions and guide service navigation.
  • Lead qualification logic to collect enquiry details before a human follows up.
  • Relevant page recommendations within the conversation to reduce drop-off.
  • Multilingual assistance to support visitors in their preferred language.
  • Booking and appointment guidance integrated with your existing contact workflow.
  • Human handoff routing so complex or unresolved queries reach the right person.
  • Enquiry collection and notification connected to your existing system.
  • Integration with your current website without replacing existing components.

Working Process

  1. Discovery: collect your most common customer questions and key service paths.
  2. Conversation design: map the flow, lead capture logic, and fallback handling.
  3. Build: implement the chatbot and connect it to your notification and enquiry system.
  4. Test: validate conversation quality, edge cases, and handoff accuracy.
  5. Launch and review: go live and refine based on early conversation data.

Who This Is Suitable For

  • Service businesses receiving repeated pre-sales enquiries that follow a pattern.
  • Companies that miss leads outside office hours.
  • Websites serving visitors in multiple languages.
  • Businesses that want to qualify leads before a human conversation.

FAQ

What can a website chatbot do?
A well-designed chatbot can answer common service questions, guide visitors to the right page, collect enquiry details, support multiple languages, and hand off to a human when needed.

Can the chatbot qualify leads before passing them on?
Yes. Lead qualification logic can be built into the conversation flow to collect service type, budget context, and contact details before a human follows up.

Does the chatbot support multiple languages?
Yes. Multilingual support can be built in, with the chatbot detecting or accepting a language preference and responding accordingly.

How does human handoff work?
When a conversation needs a human response, the chatbot collects the enquiry details and routes them to the right person via email, notification, or your existing contact system.

Is GDPR considered in chatbot design?
Data collection in the chatbot is designed to align with your privacy policy and existing consent practices. Legal compliance decisions remain with you and your legal advisors.
