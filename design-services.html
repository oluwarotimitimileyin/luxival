Questions

Frequently asked

No experience required. The construction guide is written specifically for beginners — every step explained in plain language with no assumed knowledge. The quick reference card summarises all 64 steps on one printed page.
Standard patterns come in fixed sizes like S, M, L. This pattern is calculated from your 12 body measurements — chest, waist, hips, shoulder, sleeve, back length, inseam, and more. Every piece is sized to your body, not a generic block.
After paying on SumUp, you receive a payment receipt to your email. Your custom pattern PDF is then prepared and emailed to the same address within 2 hours. The PDF contains all cutting sheets, the construction guide, grading chart, and quick reference card.
Any tailor can use it. The pattern follows standard industry conventions — grain lines, notches, seam allowances, and balance marks are all labelled. A tailor who has never seen this specific pattern can pick it up and work from it immediately.
The grading chart included in the PDF explains how to adjust each piece up or down. For a completely new set of measurements, you can purchase again at the same price and we will generate a fresh pattern for your updated measurements.
The PDF is tiled across standard A4 pages at 1:1 scale. Print all pages, then tape them together following the tile marks to assemble the full pattern pieces. A home inkjet or laser printer works fine — no specialist printing needed.
