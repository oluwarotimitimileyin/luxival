# Discovery Call Questionnaire

Use this questionnaire during the first sales call or meeting with a prospect. Adapt the order and depth based on the service requested.

## 1. Context and background

- Can you tell me a little about [CUSTOMER_NAME] and what you do?
- What prompted you to reach out to Luxival now?
- Have you worked with a similar provider before? What was the experience?

## 2. Goals and success criteria

- What does success look like for this project or service?
- Are there specific KPIs or outcomes you want to improve?
- What would happen if this problem is not solved in the next [TIMEFRAME]?

## 3. Current state

- How are you handling this today?
- What tools, platforms, or processes are already in place?
- Where are the biggest pain points or bottlenecks?

## 4. Scope and requirements

- Which Luxival service are you most interested in? [SERVICE_OPTIONS]
- Do you have a clear scope in mind, or do you need help defining it?
- Are there must-haves versus nice-to-haves?
- Do you have existing brand guidelines, designs, or technical requirements?

## 5. Budget and timeline

- Do you have a budget range in mind?
- When would you like the work to start?
- Is there a hard deadline (event, launch, regulatory, etc.)?

## 6. Decision process

- Who else is involved in the decision?
- What does your approval process look like?
- What concerns might others on your team have?

## 7. Competitors and alternatives

- Are you considering other providers or solutions?
- What would make Luxival the right choice for you?

## 8. Next steps

- Would you like to receive a formal proposal or quotation?
- What information do you need from us to move forward?
- When would be a good time to follow up?
