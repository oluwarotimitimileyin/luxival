**Base currency:** EUR (Luxival is Finland-based)

**Exchange rates used:** Approximate mid-market rates for planning purposes. Verify live rates before publishing prices in any local currency.

### Country-specific chauffeur market notes

#### France

- **Blacklane Paris:** Distance-based fixed pricing, 1 hour free wait, flight tracking.
- **Taxi benchmark:** Orly to central Paris €35–€45; private transfer to Disneyland from €117.
- **Estimated premium range:** CDG to city centre likely €90–€150 for E-Class equivalent.

#### Sweden

- **Blacklane Stockholm:** Arlanda (ARN) is 37 km from city centre.
- **Estimated premium range:** ARN to city likely SEK 1,000–1,600 for business sedan.
- **Currency:** Blacklane bills Swedish rides in SEK.

#### Netherlands

- **Blacklane Amsterdam:** Schiphol (AMS) is 18 km from city centre.
- **Estimated premium range:** AMS to city likely €80–€130 for business sedan.

#### UAE / Dubai

- **Blacklane Dubai:** DXB to Burj Khalifa area ~15 minutes.
- **Market context:** Dubai taxis are state-controlled and relatively inexpensive; premium chauffeur pricing competes on reliability and fixed rates.
- **Estimated premium range:** AED 250–450 for airport-to-city business sedan.

#### Switzerland

- **Market estimate:** Premium airport transfers in Zurich/Geneva typically CHF 120–220 for E-Class equivalent; hourly CHF 90–150.
- **Note:** High cost of living supports higher rates than Finland.

#### Canada

- **Market estimate:** Toronto Pearson to downtown business sedan CAD 120–200; hourly CAD 90–140.

#### Australia

- **Market estimate:** Sydney Airport to CBD business sedan AUD 150–250; hourly AUD 100–160.
