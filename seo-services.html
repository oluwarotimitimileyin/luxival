SEO Services for Businesses That Need Better Visibility

If search engines cannot understand your pages, potential customers will not find you. Luxival provides practical technical and on-page SEO work to improve how your site is structured, indexed, and read by search engines.

Problems This Service Solves

  • Pages are not appearing in search results for relevant service terms.
  • Important pages are missing metadata or have duplicate titles.
  • The site has no structured internal linking between related pages.
  • Structured data is missing, incorrect, or not validated.
  • Mobile usability issues are affecting search visibility.
  • Slow pages are contributing to poor Core Web Vitals scores.

What You Get

  • Technical SEO review: crawlability, indexing, canonical tags, and robots rules.
  • On-page audit: headings, metadata, keyword mapping, and page intent alignment.
  • Internal linking improvements between service and content pages.
  • Sitemap review and submission support where relevant.
  • Structured data implementation: Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, and LocalBusiness where appropriate.
  • Mobile usability check and recommendations.
  • Performance-related SEO: page speed review tied to Core Web Vitals.
  • Local visibility support for Finnish service terms and location-based queries.
  • Written action plan with prioritised fixes and rationale.

Working Process

  1. Baseline review: crawl the site, check indexing status, and map current page structure.
  2. Priority list: identify quick wins and structural issues that matter most.
  3. Implementation: apply fixes to metadata, headings, internal links, and schema.
  4. Content updates: revise page copy to align with real service intent where needed.
  5. Review: confirm fixes are in place and plan the next iteration.

FAQ

Do you guarantee first page rankings?
No. Search rankings depend on competition, domain history, and ongoing effort. We focus on practical, measurable improvements to structure and visibility.

What is the difference between technical SEO and on-page SEO?
Technical SEO covers crawlability, indexing, site speed, and structured data. On-page SEO covers headings, metadata, keyword targeting, and content structure on individual pages.

Can SEO work be done without rebuilding the site?
Yes. Many improvements can be applied to existing pages without a rebuild, especially metadata, internal linking, and structured data.

Do you help with local visibility in Finland?
Yes. Local relevance improvements include targeting Finnish service terms, improving page clarity for local queries, and ensuring consistent business information across the site.

How long before SEO improvements take effect?
Technical fixes and indexing changes can take effect within days to weeks. Ranking improvements for competitive terms take longer and depend on many factors outside our control.
