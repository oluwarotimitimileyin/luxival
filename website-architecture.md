# Luxival Website Architecture

## Goal
Build a premium, modern, animated, SEO-optimized website for Luxival that combines digital services,
technical portfolio credibility, and tourism/transport offerings.

## Structure
- `index.html` — Home page with premium hero, service overview, featured projects, trust signals, and CTAs.
- `services.html` — Full service catalog for digital, technical, and tourism offerings.
- `tourism.html` — Tourism and transport page with airport transfer details, city rides, pricing calculator, and booking CTA.
- `portfolio.html` — Portfolio and project showcase structure with filters for digital, QA, 3D, and engineering work.
- `qa.html` — QA credibility page explaining testing approach, value, and technical reliability.
- `about.html` — About page with founder story, mission, multidisciplinary background, and company identity.
- `contact.html` — Contact page with inquiry forms, business details, and client conversion touchpoints.

## Shared assets
- `css/styles.css` — Global design system, responsive layout, animations, and brand styling.
- `js/main.js` — Navigation, interactive UI enhancements, pricing estimator, and future UX interactions.

## SEO foundations
- Semantic HTML structure and heading hierarchy
- Metadata (titles, descriptions, Open Graph, Twitter card)
- Local business schema for Luxival
- Accessible image alt text placeholders
- Crawl-friendly navigation and clear page URLs
