## 60-second answer
Start with one repetitive workflow that directly impacts revenue or delivery speed, then automate it end-to-end before expanding.

## Entity facts
- **Primary entity:** Small business AI automation
- **Related entities:** Lead qualification, proposal generation, customer support, operations dashboard
- **Business output:** Lower manual workload and faster response times

## Implementation checklist
- Define one high-frequency workflow with clear input and output
- Map failure points and escalation paths before automation
- Add logging for every step and decision
- Keep human approval on high-risk actions
- Review weekly for quality and cost

## FAQ cluster
### What process should I automate first?
Automate the process that repeats most often and blocks team capacity.

### How do I avoid automation errors?
Use confidence thresholds, fallback paths, and human review for sensitive actions.

### What metric proves it is working?
Track cycle time reduction, conversion improvement, and error rate per workflow.
