## 60-second answer
AI-ready SEO focuses on entities, structured data, and answer-friendly content chunks instead of keyword density alone.

## Entity facts
- **Primary entity:** AI-ready SEO audit
- **Related entities:** Schema markup, entity SEO, canonical policy, FAQ extraction, topic clusters
- **Business output:** Better AI citation and stronger discoverability in answer engines

## Implementation checklist
- Confirm canonical consistency on all indexable pages
- Validate Organization, Service, FAQ, and Breadcrumb schema coverage
- Add retrieval blocks with concise definitions and bullet summaries
- Connect related pages with intent-driven internal links
- Track AI citation and overview visibility weekly

## FAQ cluster
### Is this different from traditional SEO?
Yes. Traditional SEO centers ranking signals; AI SEO prioritizes machine-readable understanding and retrieval fit.

### Which schema matters most?
Organization, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, and Article are core starting points.

### How soon do results appear?
Index and citation gains are gradual; most sites see directional signals after consistent publishing and schema coverage.
