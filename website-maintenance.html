Why maintain proactively

What regular maintenance prevents

Accumulated technical debt

Small issues left unresolved compound over time. A broken redirect today becomes an indexation problem in six months. Regular maintenance catches them early.

Unnoticed performance decline

Page speed degrades gradually as content grows and third-party scripts accumulate. Regular monitoring keeps Core Web Vitals within acceptable ranges.

Security vulnerabilities

Outdated dependencies and exposed endpoints create risk over time. Routine security checks identify and close vulnerabilities before they are exploited.

Content becoming outdated

Old pricing, service changes, and outdated contact details erode trust. Regular content reviews keep the site accurate and credible.

Common questions

FAQ — Website Maintenance

What does website maintenance include?

Content updates, technical health checks, performance monitoring, broken link fixes, security reviews, and keeping dependencies and third-party integrations up to date.

Do you maintain websites you did not build?

Yes. We can take over maintenance of existing websites from any provider. We conduct an initial audit before agreeing a maintenance scope.

What is the difference between one-off fixes and ongoing maintenance?

One-off fixes address a specific known issue. Ongoing maintenance is a regular engagement covering agreed tasks each month — keeping the site healthy rather than only fixing problems after they appear.
