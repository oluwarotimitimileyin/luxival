Common questions

FAQ

What does a web design project include?

Page structure planning, responsive layout, core technical SEO setup, internal link planning, content guidance, and launch support with handover notes.

How long does a web design project take?

Most small business websites take two to six weeks depending on content readiness and how quickly feedback rounds can be completed.

Do you work with existing websites?

Yes. If your current site has a workable structure, we can improve pages, speed, and conversion flow without a full rebuild.

Is SEO included in web design?

Technical SEO basics are included. Ongoing SEO is a separate service. See the SEO services page for details.
