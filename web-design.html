Web Design Services for Businesses

A business website should do one thing well: help the right visitor understand what you do and contact you. Luxival builds responsive, testable, search-friendly websites with clear enquiry paths and practical handover documentation.

Problems This Service Solves

  • Visitors leave because the site is hard to read on mobile.
  • Pages load slowly and lose potential customers before they see your offer.
  • There is no clear path from landing to enquiry or booking.
  • Search engines cannot understand the page structure, so the site stays invisible.
  • The site is difficult to update after handover.

What You Get

  • Mobile-first, responsive layout tested at all common screen sizes.
  • Clear page structure with headings, service sections, and trust signals.
  • Booking and enquiry workflows connected to your preferred contact channel.
  • SEO-ready foundations: metadata, headings, canonical tags, internal links.
  • Lightweight build with fast load times and no unnecessary dependencies.
  • Website testing before launch to catch usability and compatibility issues.
  • Post-launch support for thirty days and a handover pack with content guidance.

Working Process

  1. Discovery: review your services, audience, and current site if one exists.
  2. Page plan: agree on the pages, sections, and enquiry paths needed.
  3. Build: implement the design with regular check-ins and a feedback round.
  4. Testing: check all pages on mobile and desktop, validate forms and links.
  5. Launch: go live, confirm everything works, and hand over the project files.

Who This Is Suitable For

  • Service businesses that need a clear, professional website.
  • Companies launching a new offer and needing a focused landing page.
  • Businesses with an outdated site that is difficult to navigate on mobile.
  • Teams that want a maintainable website with documentation and support.

FAQ

Do you build websites from scratch or improve existing ones?
Both. New builds start with a clear page plan and structure. Existing sites can be improved on specific pages, flows, or speed without a full rebuild.

Is SEO included in web design?
Technical SEO foundations are included: page titles, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, canonical tags, internal linking, and clean crawlable structure.

How long does a website project take?
Most business websites take two to six weeks from brief to launch, depending on content readiness and feedback speed.

Will the site work on mobile?
Yes. All pages are built mobile-first and tested at common screen sizes from 320px upwards.

What happens after launch?
A handover package includes page notes, content guidance, and thirty days of post-launch support for fixes and questions.
