Landing Page Design for Campaigns and Lead Generation

A landing page with a clear structure, focused CTA, and working form will do more for an enquiry or campaign than a homepage that tries to cover everything. Luxival designs and builds landing pages that are responsive, search-friendly, and connected to your existing enquiry workflow.

Problems This Service Solves

  • Campaign traffic lands on the homepage instead of a page built for that specific offer.
  • Visitors arrive with intent but cannot find a clear next step.
  • Forms are disconnected from your team's existing contact process.
  • Pages work on desktop but break the layout or hide the CTA on mobile.
  • There is no SEO structure to support the page in organic search over time.

What You Get

  • A focused single-page design built around one clear action or outcome.
  • Responsive layout tested at all common screen sizes from 320px upwards.
  • CTA design and placement aligned with the page's goal.
  • Form integration connected to your existing enquiry or CRM workflow.
  • SEO-ready implementation: title, meta description, heading structure, canonical tag.
  • Structured data where relevant to the page type and content.
  • Testing before launch: forms, links, mobile display, and load behaviour.
  • Post-launch support to fix any issues that arise after the page goes live.

Working Process

  1. Brief: agree on the page goal, audience, and the one action you want visitors to take.
  2. Structure: plan the sections, copy hierarchy, and CTA placement.
  3. Build: implement the page with responsive design and form integration.
  4. Test: validate on mobile and desktop, check forms and links, and review load speed.
  5. Launch: publish, confirm SEO settings, and monitor for any early issues.

Who This Is Suitable For

  • Businesses running a campaign that needs its own focused page.
  • Companies launching a new service and wanting a dedicated lead-generation page.
  • Teams that need a page built and ready quickly without a full website rebuild.
  • Service providers whose current homepage does not support specific conversion goals.

FAQ

What is a landing page and how is it different from a website?
A landing page is a focused single page designed around one goal: getting a visitor to take a specific action. Unlike a full website, it removes distractions and guides the visitor toward one clear outcome.

Do landing pages include SEO?
Yes. Each landing page is built with a clear title, meta description, heading structure, canonical tag, and internal links as a minimum. Structured data can be added where relevant.

Can forms on the landing page connect to our existing system?
Yes. Forms are integrated with your existing contact or CRM workflow rather than creating a separate disconnected system.

Can the landing page be tested before going live?
Yes. Testing is included as part of the delivery process, covering mobile, desktop, forms, and links before launch.

Do you guarantee a specific conversion rate?
No. Conversion rates depend on your offer, audience, and traffic quality. We focus on building a clear, well-structured page that gives visitors a reason to act.
