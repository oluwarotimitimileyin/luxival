Common questions

What you might be wondering

Do I need technical knowledge to use an AI workflow?
No. We design the workflow so you use it through a simple interface, usually a form, dashboard, or messaging channel. The technical setup stays behind the scenes.
How much does it cost to run after it is built?
The main ongoing cost is API usage, which scales with how often the workflow runs. For a typical content or lead workflow, this is often around €10 to €50 per month depending on volume.
Can the workflow connect to tools I already use?
Yes. We can integrate with Supabase, Google APIs, Slack, email, Notion, Airtable, REST APIs, and most services that have a public API or webhook.
What if the workflow makes a mistake?
Workflows are built with error handling, confidence thresholds, and human review points for important decisions. You define what can be drafted or routed automatically and what must be reviewed by you.
How long does a build take?
A starter workflow takes roughly one to two weeks. A larger workflow takes three to five weeks. Enterprise systems are scoped individually.
